About Cloverville village

Hello, visitors!


Welcome to Cloverville website, here you can get to know our village more, see sustainable activities that we do and get inspired.


Cloverville

We are a tight-knit community of 42 eco-friendly folks living sustainably and supporting each other. In Cloverville, we are sharing everything from our homegrown veggies to egg cartons. We grow our own food and cycle everywhere. Imagine gardens bursting with fresh veggies and communal dinners filled with laughter and delicious homegrown food.


How do we work?

We are inspiring each other through green actions, like eating vegan or barter exchange, so everyone can get greener. However, we do not want any competition! The focus is on community, sharing tasks and trade offers. To track our efforts we are using unique point system, that also unlocks new community projects and encourages participation in communal tasks.


Cloverville is all about community spirit, nature and finding joy in simple, green things. It would be great if more of you would join the green revolution!
